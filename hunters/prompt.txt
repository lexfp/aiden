- Performance: Smooth animations, optimized rendering, responsive controls

- Hit detection
- Damage system
- Health + elimination system

Bots:
- Pathfinding
- Aim logic with adjustable accuracy
- Difficulty levels
- Unique behaviors (aggressive, defensive, tactical)

AI SYSTEM:
- Beginner bots (slow reaction, poor aim)
- Intermediate bots (balanced)
- Advanced bots (dodging, flanking, using cover)
- Bots scale in difficulty as the player progresses
