You are upgrading an existing browser-based 3D combat game. Your task is to significantly expand and improve the weapon system to feel more like Roblox Rivals: fast-paced, varied, arcade-like, and highly satisfying.

WEAPON EXPANSION GOAL:
Add a large set of new weapons (at least +15 more) that feel unique, fun, and distinct from each other. Every weapon must have a clear role and playstyle.

WEAPON DESIGN PRINCIPLES:
- Every weapon should feel different (not just stat changes)
- Focus on fun and responsiveness over realism
- Slightly exaggerated/arcade-style behavior is encouraged
- Weapons should be satisfying to use (visual + sound feedback)

NEW WEAPON TYPES TO ADD:
Include creative and Rivals-style weapons such as:
- Burst rifles (3-shot burst)
- Auto pistols (fast, low damage)
- Heavy machine guns (slow movement, high fire rate)
- Charge weapons (hold to increase damage)
- Explosive weapons (rocket launcher, grenade launcher)
- Energy weapons (laser beam, plasma shots)
- Spread weapons (wide shotgun variants)
- Melee variants (fast knife, heavy hammer, energy blade)
- Trick weapons (ricochet bullets, bouncing projectiles)
- Status weapons (slow effect, burn damage, knockback)

WEAPON MECHANICS:
Each weapon must define:
- Damage
- Fire rate
- Reload time
- Ammo capacity
- Accuracy/spread
- Recoil or kickback
- Special behavior (if applicable)

SPECIAL EFFECTS (IMPORTANT):
Make weapons feel powerful using:
- Muzzle flash effects
- Unique bullet/projectile visuals
- Impact particles
- Screen feedback (small shake, hit markers)
- Distinct sound effects per weapon

BALANCING:
- No weapon should dominate all situations
- High power weapons should have tradeoffs (slow reload, recoil, etc.)
- Cheap weapons = simple but effective
- Expensive weapons = more complex and powerful

RARITY / TIERS:
(Optional but recommended)
- Common
- Rare
- Epic
- Legendary

Higher tiers:
- Better stats OR unique mechanics (not just stronger)

SHOP INTEGRATION:
- Add all new weapons to the shop
- Price them based on effectiveness and uniqueness
- Ensure progression feels rewarding

LOADOUT COMPATIBILITY:
- Allow new weapons to be equipped in existing loadout system
- Ensure no UI breaks

AI UPDATE:
- Bots should be able to use some of the new weapons
- Advanced bots can use more complex weapons effectively

CODE REQUIREMENTS:
- Keep code modular and organized
- Use reusable weapon classes or objects
- Avoid duplicating logic
- Make it easy to add more weapons later

FINAL GOAL:
The weapon system should feel:
- Fast
- Fun
- Varied
- Visually satisfying
- Closer to Roblox Rivals gameplay

Do not remove existing features—only expand and improve the weapon system.
